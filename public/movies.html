Three best movies according to Samridhi

My top picks of all time

3 Idiots

This movie tells us about how to live life in a different way. Chosing our passion over the burden which we all faced all our life , living our life to the fullest and not taking anything seriously.

Yeh jawani hai deewani

This movie is not only a source of entertainment, but also given a great lesson about life and making good friends. My favourite character in this movie is naina.

A silent voice

This movie is an anime. Which tells us a lesson on how our past mistakes can effect our future and our mental health, This movie gives us the message on how bulling can affect a student's life and can give a life long mental trauma.
